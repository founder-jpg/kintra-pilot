The Villa

In the heart of Seminyak — within walking distance of the beach, the best restaurants on the island, and Bali's most celebrated boutiques — Villa Kundalini occupies a sprawling 1,600m² estate designed for groups who want to do it properly. Six bedrooms, a private pool, a tropical garden, and space for up to 18 guests.

The indoor living areas open seamlessly to the garden and pool, creating the kind of indoor-outdoor flow that only a villa of this scale can deliver. A convertible massage room adds flexibility — it becomes a seventh bedroom when the group needs it.
