The Villa

In a quiet residential pocket of Bophut, where frangipani leans over whitewashed walls and the morning air carries something floral and still — Baan Jasmine sits at the end of a small lane, giving nothing away from the street.

Step through the gate and the world changes. A 900m² walled tropical garden unfolds around a generous Bali-stone pool, all shaded salas, sun terraces, and the kind of privacy that feels increasingly rare on this island. The villa has been both a cherished private home and a successful boutique holiday property for over a decade — a testament to how well it lives.

Both double bedrooms occupy opposite ends of the villa for maximum privacy, each with en-suite bathrooms and garden-facing balconies that step directly down to the pool. High ceilings, ceiling fans, and open-plan living spaces flow naturally toward the covered outdoor dining area — a home designed for the Samui pace, where indoors and outdoors are always one conversation.

The 900m² plot is also a quiet opportunity. With land of this size in one of Samui's most coveted residential districts, there is genuine scope for a guest pavilion, additional living accommodation, or expansion over time — subject to planning.
