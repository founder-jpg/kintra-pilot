Rental inquiries to viewings, faster

AI tools that help rental teams reply faster and book more viewings — inside WhatsApp.

Rental pilot • Italy — 2–4 weeks. No risk if it doesn’t save time.

The Problem

Rental teams drown in WhatsApp, email, and portals. Threads fragment, follow-ups slip, and reply times creep up just as lead volume spikes.

  • Slow replies = lost, impatient tenants.
  • No central thread = missed context.
  • Managers can’t see today’s pulse in one view.

Fragmented channels → slower decisions and more churn.

The Kintra rental solution

Every WhatsApp inquiry lands in one thread; Kintra drafts the reply in Italian and surfaces the next action — while managers see a live pulse of today’s leads.

Faster replies: Instant draft in Italian with two time slots ready to send.
Nothing slips: Suggestions like “send planimetria” or “confirm reminder” with each draft.
Manager view: Today’s WhatsApp leads, reply times, and bookings in one dashboard.

WhatsApp-native for agents, dashboard-ready for managers.

Rental pilot plan (2–4 weeks)

Week 1 — Connect: WhatsApp Cloud + web inbox. Rental reply flow enabled for one team.
Week 2 — Run live: Real tenant inquiries routed through Kintra, agents approve & send.
Week 3 — Measure: Minutes saved per agent; reply time vs baseline; bookings per 10 leads.
Week 4 — Decide: Roll forward, expand to more agents, or stop. No lock-in.
Why this matters
Saving ~2 min per reply × 20 rental leads ≈ 40 minutes/day/agent. Across a team, that’s hours of extra capacity each week.
