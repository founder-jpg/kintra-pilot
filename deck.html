The rental inbox problem

Rental agents spend hours every day juggling WhatsApp, portals, and email. Threads fragment, tenants chase updates, and managers can’t see what’s really happening.

  • High-volume WhatsApp chats for rentals — no structure, no priorities.
  • Agents re-type the same answers for availability, price, and documents.
  • Slow replies = lost tenants and empty days in your calendar.

High inquiry volume, low visibility → slower decisions and more manual work.

The Kintra rental solution
