The Villa

Perched 65 metres above Haad Salad Bay, Tramonto Rosso — Italian for Red Sunset — is a sunset ocean retreat where warm Mediterranean tones meet Thai island luxury. White walls, bold red accents, and carefully curated art pieces create an interior that feels as dramatic as the views it frames.

The villa faces north-east, meaning every evening the Gulf of Thailand turns the same deep crimson as the name promises. From the infinity pool edge — 29 sqm of salt-treated water hovering over the coastline — the horizon stretches unbroken to the edge of the world.
