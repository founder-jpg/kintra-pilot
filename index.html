Questions we hear

Why not just use ChatGPT?

Generic chat tools don’t see your lead history, listing data, or WhatsApp thread context. Kintra sits in the flow: it detects intent in real messages, pulls listing facts, drafts compliant replies, suggests the next step, and logs outcomes to your dashboard—so managers see the daily pulse.

Is the pilot risky?

It’s time-boxed (30 days), scoped to one niche, and measured on 2–3 KPIs. If it doesn’t save time, don’t continue. You keep the learning and assets.

Will this replace agents?

No. It speeds up routine replies and keeps follow-ups from slipping. Agents approve and send; managers get clarity; clients get faster service.

WhatsApp compliance?

Production uses the official WhatsApp Business API (Meta Cloud). Pilots use a test number; we rate-limit demo sends to prevent abuse.
