WhatsApp Smart Reply

Before / After view of real conversations – with Client, Kintra, Agent, Insight and Next step.

Before – standard rental reply

Client

«Buongiorno, l'appartamento in Via Roma è ancora disponibile? Posso vederlo questa settimana?»

Agent

«Sì, è disponibile. Quando è libero?» [reply sent hours later]

Insight: Tenant does the work, no next step.

After – Kintra-assisted rental reply

Client

«Buongiorno…»

Kintra (draft)

«Ciao [Nome], sì, l'appartamento è disponibile.

· 2 camere, 1 bagno
· Disponibile da [data]

Mer 18:30 o Gio 17:00 per la visita—quale preferisci?»

Agent (send)

Agent reviews and sends in seconds.

Insight: Clear options, fast reply.
Next step: Viewing booked.

Before – sales lead in WhatsApp

Client

“Hi, is the apartment still available?”

Agent

“Yes, when are you free?” [late reply]

Insight: No urgency, weak next step.

After – Kintra-assisted sales reply

Client

“Hi, is it still available?”

Kintra (draft)

“Hi [Name], yes it’s available.

Wed 18:30 or Thu 17:00—what works best?”

Agent (send)

Agent checks and sends.

Insight: Protects sales leads before they go cold.
