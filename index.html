Questions we hear

Why is the pilot paid?

It funds setup, live support, and measurable outcomes in 30 days. Free trials drift; paid pilots focus. Most teams recoup the fee from time saved alone.

How soon do we see ROI?

Typically within the pilot window. Faster replies + suggested next steps reduce leakage and manager chase time. See “Show assumptions” above for the math.

Why not just use ChatGPT?

Generic chat tools don’t live in your WhatsApp thread, don’t see listing context or lead history, and don’t suggest next steps tied to your workflow. Kintra does all three, in one place.

What exactly happens after 30 days?

We review KPIs and time-saved, decide together, and—if it’s working—expand to more agents/listings. If it’s not a fit, you stop. No lock-in.

Will it work with our CRM (e.g., Getrix)?

Pilot path: light CSV/API bridge for listings & tags; production path adds deeper sync. We keep scope narrow in pilot to prove value quickly.

How do you handle data & privacy?

We use WhatsApp’s official Cloud API and minimize data retention during pilot. Your data remains yours; production uses your verified WABA and hardened storage policies.
