Questions we hear (and straight answers)

Why not just use ChatGPT or a generic bot?
General chatbots don’t see your listings, calendars, or past threads. Kintra sits on your WhatsApp + CRM, drafts in context, suggests next steps, and tracks outcomes in a manager dashboard.
What exactly is “1 niche, 2–3 KPIs”?
We focus on one slice of your business (e.g., rentals in a zone, or premium listings) and agree 2–3 simple success measures (e.g., reply-time, booked viewings, listing reach). It keeps the pilot fast and measurable.
Is €1.5–2k fair for a pilot?
It’s ~one junior monthly cost, but value compounds across the whole team via faster replies and clearer next-step discipline. If we don’t save time in 30 days, don’t continue — no hard feelings.
Security & data?
We use Meta’s Cloud API for WhatsApp and follow least-privilege access. Pilots run in a sandbox; production uses your verified WABA. We never sell your data.
What happens after the pilot?
We review outcomes on day ~25. If targets are met, we propose a rollout plan (seats, training, SLAs). If not, we stop — you keep the insights and workflow maps.
