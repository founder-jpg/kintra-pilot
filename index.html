Questions we hear

Why not just use ChatGPT or a generic chatbot?
Generic chat tools don’t know your listings, lead history, or Italian messaging nuance. Kintra reads the context (intent, property, prior replies), drafts native-Italian responses with two time slots, and always proposes a next step (e.g., send planimetria, confirm reminder). Less tab-switching, fewer dropped balls.
How is the pilot priced and what do we actually get?
A 30-day paid pilot (€1.5–2k). We focus on one niche (e.g., residential lettings) and agree 2–3 success metrics (reply time, visits booked, pipeline speed). You get setup, dashboards, WhatsApp Smart Reply, Listing Summary cards, plus light guidance. If it doesn’t save time, don’t continue.
Will this work with our current tools (e.g., CRM, portals)?
Yes. We’re designed to sit alongside your CRM/portals. During the pilot we keep scope tight: lead capture → WhatsApp reply → visit booked → dashboard. No rip-and-replace; we start where value appears fastest.
Is data private and compliant?
We follow least-privilege access and store only what’s needed for the pilot. WhatsApp Cloud API is Meta-hosted; production runs on your verified business account. We’re happy to sign a pilot DPA.
